Pokédex Maker

My goal with the Pokédex Maker is to simplify the process of creating and mantaining a Fakémon Pokédex. I am planning to include many features, among which are: adding aspects to each Pokémon (name, types, stats,...), and organizing the Pokémon by different criteria like name, Pokédex number, etc. Also I plan on uploading every existing Pokémon to the website, so people can freely add them to their Dexes.
